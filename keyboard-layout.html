Other combinations

There are a couple of same-hand key combinations to enable switching between apps, windows and files:
  • Apps: ⇥ + R and ⇥ + E
  • Windows of the same app: ⇪ + F and ⇪ + D
  • Tabs: [ + U and [ + I
